How do I add or remove a Fee?

Navigate to the admin/fees area of OpenCounter via the top menu. You can add a new fee by clicking on the "New Fee" button on the bottom of the page. You can edit any fee by clicking "edit" and delete any fee by clicking the red "delete" button.
