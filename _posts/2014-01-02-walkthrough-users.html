Walkthrough: The "Users" Area

A User is a member of your organization that uses OpenCounter. Departments are groups of Users that take ownership of different permit applications, or sections of the application process.

Users

Users are the staff that will be administering and using OpenCounter. Users can monitor Applications, respond to Help Requests on behalf of their Department, and receive Weekly Metrics e-mails. They can also download application data, generate Department-specific forms, and edit the configuration of the permit process your organization shows the public.

Departments

Departments reflect the Departments within your enterprise that are using the OpenCounter system.

Each Page that a permit applicant sees is associated with one or more Department. As a result, Users that are members of Departments will recieve relevant information from Applications, and Applicants will be able to seek assistance from the appropriate staff members within your organization.
