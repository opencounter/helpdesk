How do I add or update the questions we ask permit applicants?

By navigating to the admin/pages page, you can edit any element of what the public sees on that page including when it's serves, and what questions it askes. Click "Add Field" button to add any question. Fields show up in a drop down menu, navigate to the one you'd like to add and then click "Save" at the bottom to make your changes permanent.
