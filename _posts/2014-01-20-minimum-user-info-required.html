What is the minimum amount of information that end-users need to provide to use OpenCounter?

Most permits require at least a few pieces of information, but we recommend that you limit the number of Fields you mark as ""required"" to only the must-haves. As a rule of thumb, the following pieces of information often constitute the bare minimum: 1. General Applicant contact data (Name, Phone, Address) 2. email Address 3. Business Name 4. Prospective Business Address 5. Use Code for the Prospective Business
