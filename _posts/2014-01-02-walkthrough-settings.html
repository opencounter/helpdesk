Walkthrough: The "Settings" Area

This basic information helps differentiate your organization’s implementation of OpenCounter from others. It helps citizens know they are in the right place---and how to get to City Hall.

This page also contains important information regarding OpenCounter’s use of your organization’s GIS server, Google Analytics, and CNAME configuration.

You may want to e-mail OpenCounter staff before editing the settings on this page. The current configuration of these fields are key to the current operation of the OpenCounter system. We are here to help!
